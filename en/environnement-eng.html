OVH's environmental impact


By offering cloud hosting solutions, OVH is contributing to the dematerialisation of IT infrastructures, reducing the need for physical servers and energy consumption in businesses. Nevertheless, the massive use of data centres presents environmental challenges. To meet these challenges, OVH has decided to focus on energy efficiency by developing environmentally-friendly data centres, using innovative cooling systems such as water cooling, which consumes less energy than traditional methods.
